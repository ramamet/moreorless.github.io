Information Collection

More Or Less is a nutrition education game designed to help you learn about food calories in a fun, interactive way. We are committed to protecting your privacy:

  • No personal information collected: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • No account required: You can play the game without creating an account or logging in.
  • No tracking: We do not track your behavior, location, or device information.
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics services that collect user data.

Data Storage

The only data stored by More Or Less is your game progress, which is saved locally on your device:

  • High scores: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Game preferences: Sound settings and difficulty mode are saved locally.
  • No cloud sync: Your data never leaves your device.
  • Complete control: You can clear this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

More Or Less does NOT use any third-party services that collect user data, including:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics platforms
  • No social media integrations
  • No crash reporting services that collect personal data

The app is completely offline and operates entirely on your device.

Children's Privacy

More Or Less is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we are compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
